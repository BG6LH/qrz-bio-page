Welcome to BG6LH's QRZ biopage!

I am living in ON80fa, Beijing, China. I am a product manager and programmer for Internet service. I became a HAM in 1996, and got my callsign in 1998, and my first radio license in 2007.

In most of past years, I was interested in VHF/UHF radio. However, the FT8 mode take me back into HF world, in 2018. In the summer of 2021, I became a Class C operator of CRAC.

My Radio Station

  • HF：FTDX10 & SCU-LAN10 / FT-818ND
  • V/UHF：FT3DR / FTM-100D
  • And some HF Kit：QCX mini 40m / USDX 4020 / D4D 4020 / Pixie etc.
  • Antenna: EFHW4010, GAWANT, Small Loop, Random Wire with Elecraft T1.

FTDX10

That's my home station now, a small radio cabinet on my balcony, a FTDX10, working with a Raspberry Pi 4B, an EFHW4010 antenna, 20 meters high above ground.

FT-818ND

That is my mobile QRP station, an FT-818ND, working with some portable antennas and two homebrew small magnetic loop antennas.

Although FT-81x has been discontinued, I still love this twenty years selling product. I design a Bluetooth DIGI adapter. I can be mounted on the rear panel.

This is an open source project, sharing on my Github: https://github.com/BG6LH/FT-81x-BT-DIGI-Adaptor
